Here's that same scene with indirect lighting using photon mapping enabled and different settings for the photon mapper.

Note now the entire rest of the room is visible! The big issue with this technique is the noise (notice how the higher photon count samples are super noisy. This could be fixed with denoising algorithms such as gaussian blurring to cover them up.
